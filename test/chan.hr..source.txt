		For almost <b>forty years</b> we have been pioneers in the promotion of Buddhist values and teaching in the troubled lands of the <b>former Yugoslavia</b>, beset by discord and conflicts. We started as a small group of enthusiasts meeting at private homes. Twenty years ago we established the first educational Buddhist institution in <b>Croatia</b>, a Buddhist centre in Zagreb, the nation's capital, fitted with a study library and a meditation hall. Today, <b>Dharmaloka Chan Buddhist Community</b> is on the verge of fulfilling its long-standing vision of building a <b>retreat centre</b>, a place dedicated to the cultivation of inner and outer peace, deeper understanding and enlightened care for all people and the world we inhabit. It is, however, a step that we cannot accomplish on our own. <br>
